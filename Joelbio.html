Joel Rudin

Joel Rudin, violist, a former member of the Laurentian String Quartet, toured the United States, Canada, and Asia, and recorded for the Musical Heritage Society, Soundspells Productions, and Newport Classics.An active chamber musician, he has been a frequent guest artist with the All Seasons Chamber Players, di.vi.sion piano trio, both live and in the recording studio, and numerous other ensembles. For over a decade, Joel was violist of The New York Five, a mixed chamber ensemble dedicated to the music of composer George Quincy, as heard on Albany and Cottage Records. More recently he is a member of the Allentown Chamber Players. Mr. Rudin has played with the New York Philharmonic, New Jersey Symphony, American Symphony, and many others, as well as on Broadway. He is currently principal viola of Orchestra Long Island and the Symphony of Westchester and was formerly principal viola of the New York Grand Opera and the Delaware Valley Philharmonic. Joel is a frequent guest artist with the Alborada Spanish Dance Theatre.
